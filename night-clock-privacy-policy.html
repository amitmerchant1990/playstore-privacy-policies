Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2025

Night Clock (Always On) is built by AmitMerchant.com with simplicity and privacy in mind.

No Data Collected

This app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or non-personal data. Everything runs locally on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no background data usage—zero.

Local-Only Functionality

All features and settings of the app work entirely on your Android device. Internet access is not required, and no data ever leaves your phone. Your information stays yours—always.

Child-Friendly

Night Clock (Always On) is designed to be safe for all users, including children. It contains no ads, no external links, and no in-app purchases. There is no content that could be considered inappropriate or unsafe for younger audiences.

Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, your privacy and security are inherently protected. The app does not interact with remote servers, ensuring peace of mind for all users.

Changes to This Policy

Although we don't expect this privacy approach to change, if we ever introduce features that affect your privacy, this policy will be updated here and clearly communicated through app updates.
